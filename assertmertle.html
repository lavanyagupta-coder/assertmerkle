About Us

AssetMerkle is the official technical society of IGDTUW, dedicated to fostering innovation and learning in Web3 and Blockchain technologies.

Web3 is the next generation of the internet, where applications are decentralized and powered by blockchain technology. It allows users to have control over their data, interact with decentralized apps (dApps), and participate in decentralized finance (DeFi), NFTs, and other blockchain-based ecosystems.

Blockchain is a secure, distributed ledger technology that records information in a transparent and immutable way. Each block contains a record of transactions, linked cryptographically to the previous one, ensuring security, trust, and decentralization without the need for a central authority.

At AssetMerkle, we organize:

  • Hackathons - to challenge and nurture problem-solving skills in real-time projects.
  • Workshops - hands-on sessions to learn blockchain, smart contracts, and Web3 development.
  • Collaborations - opportunities to work with industry experts and startups in the blockchain ecosystem.

Our mission is to empower students to explore, build, and innovate in the world of decentralized technologies. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced developer, AssetMerkle offers the resources and community to grow in Web3.

AssetMerkle is always open to collaborate and grow with like-minded individuals, organizations, and communities. We welcome partnerships that help us explore new ideas, create innovative solutions, and expand the Web3 and blockchain ecosystem together.

Discover Web3 & Blockchain

What is Web3?

Web3 is the next generation of the internet, built on decentralization. Instead of companies controlling your data, you own it and interact directly through blockchain-powered apps.

Think of it like a giant treehouse 🌳. In the old internet (Web2), one kid had the key 🔑 and decided who could enter. In Web3, everyone has their own key, and you keep your toys (data, coins, art) safe in your locker. No one can take them away, and you can even carry them to other treehouses! 🚀

What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a secure, distributed digital ledger that records transactions in a transparent and tamper-proof way. Every block stores data and is linked to the one before it, making the chain unbreakable and trustworthy.

Imagine it like a magical class diary 📔. Every time someone writes in it, the page locks forever 🔒 and the whole class gets a copy. If one kid tries to change “I gave 2 chocolates” into “I gave 20 chocolates,” the rest will laugh and say: “Nope, our diaries say 2!” 🍫😂
